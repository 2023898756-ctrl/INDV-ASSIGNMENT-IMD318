Experience

The final year students are compulsory to partake the Industrial Training during the semester break on the fourth semester where I undergone mine at Seberang Jaya Hospital. It has been a new experience for I have never involve myself in a professional setting. The first week was a real struggle to cope however, staffs there are like a family where they are sincere in knowledge sharing and kind-hearted that they spoil me with loads of foods, drinks and gifts. Not to forget, I have two sweet and chaotic partners; Adlin Hanis and Kak Yana (coursemate and hospital's staff), who have made this journey endurable yet a special one. They both made the internship experience two times more fun and despite that, I learned a lot from this opportunity and the girls, themselves
